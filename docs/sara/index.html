Rahu sits in your 2nd house in Scorpio — the house of speech, of accumulated wealth, of the voice as inheritance. Today's transiting Cancer Moon activates your 10th house (the house of karma-phala, the fruits of action) and opposes your natal Moon position — a tension between the home that grounds you (4th house) and the world's eye upon you (10th house). This is a familiar axis for you: the farm versus the stage, the private self versus the public artist. Today's sky asks you not to choose but to hold both, knowing that the root nourishes the flower that is seen.
