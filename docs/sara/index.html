Her natal Jupiter in Virgo (10th House) grounds this expansion in craft, in the particular, in the work itself. Whatever is said today can land with unexpected abundance. Let the words move.

Rahu at the gate of the second house

Libra ASC · Vishakha Virgo Sun · Hasta Rahu Mahadasha Mars Conjunct Lagna Capricorn Moon · U. Ashadha

Rahu sits in her 2nd House in Scorpio — the house of vāk, of voice, of the accumulated wealth that an artist builds not only in coin but in repertoire, in reputation, in the particular sound that can only come from her. Rahu is the node of worldly desire, of reaching toward what feels just beyond grasp, and in this house it has been the engine of her artistic livelihood for years. The Rahu Mahadasha, which runs approximately until 2028, has been a period of amplification and shadow both: the world-stage hunger is real, the disorientation is also real. Today the transiting sky in sidereal terms brings a Moon resonant with her natal Capricorn Moon's territory — grounded, enduring, willing to labor. This is a working day in the dharmic sense: not glamorous, but load-bearing.

Mars debilitated in Libra (Vishakha nakshatra) sits conjunct her Libra Ascendant — this is one of the most complex signatures in her chart. The warrior planet in the sign of the scales loses some of its directional force, but it does not lose its fire. Instead, it becomes available for something subtler: the discipline of the artist who contains enormous intensity and chooses, daily, which form it takes. The body as instrument, the painter's arm, the musician's breath-control — all are expressions of this debilitated Mars that has learned to work through beauty rather than against it. Today, with the sidereal sky activating Libran qualities through the transiting Moon, this natal signature is invited to move with the day rather than against it.

The nakshatra of the day carries the quality of Uttara Ashadha — the Sun-ruled asterism of her natal Capricorn Moon — an energy of final victory through sustained effort, of the task that does not finish in a day but compounds over seasons. Her natal Saturn in Capricorn (own sign, 4th House) steadies this: the roots are deep, the home is the foundation, and whatever is tended on the land or within the studio today is tended in alignment with her deepest karma. Ketu in Taurus (8th House) reminds her that what is being released in this Rahu period is an older attachment to security — the node pair is pulling her toward the unfamiliar and the voiced, away from what has already been mastered and hoarded.
