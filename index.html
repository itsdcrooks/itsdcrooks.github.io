Your Sun and Mercury in Gemini in the 7th house carry the nakshatra of Ardra — ruled by Rahu, symbolized by a teardrop, associated with the storm-god Rudra. Ardra represents the grief that precedes transformation, the destruction that clears the field for something new. With Rahu as the ruler of both your Ardra Sun and your Mahadasha, there is a recurring Vedic instruction in your chart: you are meant to work at the edge of dissolution and renewal, in others and in yourself. The Venus antardasha asks you to do this with beauty and with grace — not to bludgeon transformation into being, but to seduce it. To make the healing encounter so safe, so aesthetically true, that what was hidden wants to surface on its own.
