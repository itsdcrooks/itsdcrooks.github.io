Rahu in your 5th house in Aries carries the hunger of a warrior who has never been satisfied: creativity, children, dharmic intelligence, romance — these are the territories Rahu inflames in you, amplifying desire until ordinary ambition feels insufficient. Venus, your antardasha ruler, sits in the 8th house in Cancer, in Ashlesha nakshatra — the serpentine, penetrating energy that knows how to wait, how to coil, how to extract truth from depth. Swati is ruled by Vayu, the wind god — it carries independence, adaptability, the capacity to move with larger forces rather than against them. The Swati Moon today invites you to receive the winds of this dasha period rather than navigate them by force of will alone. Rahu is too large for willpower to contain. It requires surrender in the direction of your growth.

With your Sagittarius Ascendant ruled by Jupiter — which sits in Aries in the 5th house, in Bharani nakshatra — the dharma available to you is fundamentally about creative truth-telling and the courage to teach from lived experience rather than abstraction. Jupiter in Bharani carries the shakti of apana — the downward-moving force that processes, eliminates, and gestates. Your growth in this Rahu-Venus period is less about adding credentials and more about alchemizing what you've already survived into transmission. The hidden (8th house Venus) is becoming visible. This is the nature of Rahu's unraveling: it strips the costume away until what remains is too real to perform.
