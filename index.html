Swati is the nakshatra of the sword of air, of independence, of things that sway but do not break. The reed bends in the storm and remains rooted. In the Rahu-Venus period, the invitation is to bring that Swati quality — independence within connection, movement within rootedness — directly into the domain that Venus rules: desire, relationship, and the terms on which you offer your gifts to the world.
