Rahu Mahadasha running through Rahu-Venus antardasha places two of the most appetite-driven forces in the chart in active conversation. Venus in the 8th house (Cancer) carries the antardasha energy into hidden chambers — transformation, inheritance, the eros that lives beneath the surface. The transiting Moon moves through sidereal Scorpio today before reaching Sagittarius, activating your 12th house (from Sagittarius Ascendant). Your natal Moon in Libra (Swati nakshatra, ruled by Rahu) already lives at the intersection of social mind and spiritual detachment. Swati is the wind that uproots and yet makes possible new growth. On a Sunday under Yellow Flame, that wind blows through the unconscious — clearing, not destroying.

With natal Jupiter in Aries (5th house, Bharani nakshatra) and Rahu conjunct in that same house, the Rahu-Venus period amplifies the 5th house in both directions: brilliant creative ambition on one side, obsessive hunger on the other. Bharani nakshatra is ruled by Yama, the god of death and dharma — it governs what is carried across the threshold, what is released, what must be earned before it is given. The dharmic instruction of this antardasha is simple: do not consume what is not yet ripe. The work you are building has long roots. Trust the depth of the soil.
