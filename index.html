Today the Moon in Sagittarius transits in sidereal terms through your 1st house of self, dharma, and physical embodiment. Your natal Moon in Libra in the 11th house (Swati nakshatra — also ruled by Rahu) is receiving a trine from this transiting Sagittarius Moon, linking your aspirations and community belonging to your core identity. Some question about your place in a larger network — professional, creative, dharmic — may press gently against your awareness today. Do not try to solve it. Let it inform.

Your Sun and Mercury both occupy Ardra nakshatra in Gemini in the 7th house — Ardra is ruled by Rahu and presided over by Rudra, the storm deity who destroys in order to renew. With your Mahadasha lord Rahu so intimately connected to your Sun's nakshatra, this is a period when your core identity is being systematically dismantled and recast. This is not pathology; it is dharmic schedule. The 7th house involvement means that this transformation is partly accomplished through encounter — through the mirror of the other, whether that other is a client, a partner, a rival, or an idea that challenges everything you thought you had settled. Today's Mercury-Pluto tension in the Western sky finds its Vedic echo here: the grahas are conspiring to ensure you cannot stay comfortable in yesterday's self. Be grateful. It means the karma is moving.
