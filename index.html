You move through this period under Rahu Mahadasha with Venus as antardasha lord — a pairing that in Jyotish is often described as the amplification of worldly desire through an occult lens. Rahu sits in your 5th house in Aries, the house of purva punya — past-life merit, creativity, children, speculation, and the joy that arises from genuine expression. When these two grahas share the same temporal season, the karma being worked is precisely this: can you desire without grasping? Can you create without needing the creation to validate you?

Today's transiting Moon moves through sidereal Gemini — activating your 7th house, the house of partnership, public self, and the mirror of relationship. Your natal Sun and Mercury both reside here in Ardra nakshatra, ruled by Rahu himself. Ardra is the nakshatra of the storm — its symbol is a teardrop, its deity Rudra, the howling wind of transformation. There is a particular quality of intelligence that lives in Ardra: it sees through surfaces, it does not rest in easy consensus, and it transforms grief into understanding. Today, with the Moon transiting this same field, the relational mirror is especially clear. What others reflect back to you is not noise; it is data about where Rahu's hunger is still unresolved.

Venus in your natal 8th house in Cancer, conjunct Mars in Ashlesha nakshatra, speaks to a desire nature that is drawn toward depth, transformation, and the hidden dimensions of intimacy and resource. Ashlesha's serpent energy — coiling, perceptive, and psychologically acute — makes your Venus not comfortable with surface pleasure alone. The Rahu-Venus antardasha is bringing what was coiled into the 8th house slowly toward visibility. Something you have been building quietly — a creative vision, an entrepreneurial form, a way of offering your integrative gifts to the world — is in the process of becoming expressible. The 5th house Rahu is the destined amplifier; the 8th house Venus is the source material. Today is a day to let the material move upward.
