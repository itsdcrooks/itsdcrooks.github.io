The Rahu-Venus antardasha in which you currently travel is one of the most complex and generative passages a chart can hold. Rahu sits in your 5th House in Aries — the house of creative intelligence, past-life wisdom, and the gateway to children, speculative ventures, and the deep satisfaction of original thought. It is conjunct in sign with natal Jupiter, amplifying its hunger for expansion beyond ordinary boundaries. Venus, as antardasha lord, sits in your 8th House in Cancer — the house of transformation, hidden wealth, deep emotional excavation, and the mysteries that only intimacy can unlock. Together these two grahas are pulling you between visible desire and veiled transformation, between what you hunger to create and what life is asking you to release in order to create it. Today's Balsamic Moon (falling in Taurus in the sidereal framework, activating your 6th House of service and daily work through your Sagittarius ascendant) asks you to honor both impulses without forcing resolution.

Your Gemini Sun in the 7th House in Ardra nakshatra — ruled by Rahu — means the mahadasha lord is also the nakshatra lord of your Sun. This is a period in which your core identity, your very sense of who you are in relationship to others, is being storm-washed by Rahu's cyclonic intelligence. Ardra is the nakshatra of the tempest that clears — tears before clarity, the howl before the silence. Your natal Mercury also sits in Gemini in Ardra: the mind and the identity share the same nakshatra lord, the same destabilizing-and-liberating force. Today, with transiting Mercury forming difficult minor aspects in the Western sky, this Rahu-Mercury-Sun complex in your 7th House is activated in the domain of partnerships and public self. Your relationships are currently the primary theater in which your dharma is being refined.

Your natal Moon in Libra in Swati nakshatra — also ruled by Rahu — completes the picture. Swati is the nakshatra of the lone shoot bending in the wind, the image of the solitary being who survives not by rigidity but by flexibility. It sits in your 11th House of community, large aspirations, and social networks. The Moon here is exalted in disposition and reflects an innate gift for social attunement — but Ketu opposing it from the 5th House introduces a karmic tension between creative individuation and collective belonging. Today, as the Moon moves through Taurus in the sky (sidereal 6th House for your Sagittarius ascendant), it activates the service axis of your chart. The dharmic invitation is this: to offer your gifts without losing your center, to bend like Swati's grass without uprooting yourself. The karma of this cycle asks you to serve from fullness, not from the fear of irrelevance.
