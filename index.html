A conversation today could leave both parties more whole.

Together, Rahu and Venus are pulling you toward a creative and relational destiny that requires you to go where you have not gone before — into the uncharted, the vulnerable, the revolutionary. What you desire and what transforms you are becoming the same force.

Your natal Moon in Libra in the 11th house sits in Swati nakshatra, whose presiding deity is Vayu, the wind god, and whose symbol is a young seedling in a gust of wind — the soul that bends but does not break, that moves with karmic currents rather than against them. The grahas ask you to name what Swati already knows in the wind: what is truly reciprocal in your relationships, and what must be released for the seedling to stand.

Your natal Sun and natal Mercury both occupy Gemini in the 7th house in Ardra nakshatra — the nakshatra of Rudra, the storm, the teardrop, the fierce compassion that burns away what is no longer needed. Ardra is ruled by Rahu, and in your Rahu Mahadasha, this natal Ardra Sun is particularly alive. You are in a period when your core identity — your solar dharma — is being refined through Rahu's turbulence. The fire in those golden eyes in today's image is Ardra fire: it sees clearly because it has already wept, because it has been through something. Today, that intelligence is available to you in service. As a counselor and as an entrepreneur, the storm you have survived becomes the lantern you carry for others. This is the dharmic instruction of Rahu-Venus through Ardra: your wound is your curriculum.
