Swati nakshatra, where your natal Moon resides in Libra in the 11th house, is ruled by Rahu and governed by Vayu — the wind deity. This is appropriate. Rahu-Venus periods are famous for generating desire before clarity; the task of Jyotish wisdom is to learn to follow the desire intelligently rather than suppressing it or being consumed by it.
