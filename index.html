In this antardasha, the Vedic teaching is that your relational and creative life is the very instrument of your soul's evolution — not incidental to your dharma, but central to it.

The transiting Moon today moves through Cancer in sidereal terms, transiting your 8th house — the house of Venus and Mars in your natal chart.
