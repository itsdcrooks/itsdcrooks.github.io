That distinction is the whole lesson.

VENUS IN LEO · MOON THROUGH TAURUS

Your natal Venus in Leo in the 7th house does not love quietly. It loves with theater, with warmth, with the full expectation of being witnessed in return. The approaching Venus-Chiron square will test this later. Something in the field of relationship or partnership may surface a memory of love that was offered but not received. This is not the same as love being unavailable. It is the scar tissue mapping where you once gave beyond your means.
