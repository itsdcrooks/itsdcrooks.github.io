Let someone see the warmth in you without immediately redirecting it outward. Receive, for once, the light you so freely give.

Jupiter here is both teacher and judge: he asks what you are creating with the gifts you have been given, and whether your creative output is aligned with dharma. Today, with the Moon's transit through the 5th activating this stellium of Rahu and Jupiter in Aries, a creative reckoning quietly occurs. It need not be dramatic. Saturn retrograde in Aquarius in your 3rd house of communication and courage adds a layer of revision — something you said, wrote, or communicated recently may require a second look, a correction, a deepening. Honor that impulse. Saturn retrograde is not punishment; it is an invitation to do the work with more precision.
