Rahu's Hunger Meets Venus at the Hidden Door

SAG ASC GEMINI SUN 7H LIBRA MOON 11H RAHU DASHA VENUS ANTARDASHA

You move through the world today under the double sovereignty of Rahu and Venus — two grahas whose combined nature is neither comfortable nor simple. Rahu, the shadow planet of insatiable desire, sits in your 5th house in Aries in the Bharani nakshatra, a star whose deity is Yama, lord of death and transformation. Bharani is the nakshatra of those who carry great burdens, who stand at thresholds between creation and destruction, who hold what cannot yet be born into the world. Rahu here has been amplifying your creative drives and your appetite for recognition — but Bharani asks: can you carry the weight of your own potential without flinching? Venus, your antardasha lord, occupies your 8th house in Cancer in Ashlesha nakshatra — the domain of the hidden, the transformative, the deeply psychological. This is not a superficial Venus period. The beauty and connection and value that Venus offers you are arriving through what is concealed, through depth work, through the therapeutic relationship itself as a site of alchemy.

Today's transiting Moon moves through a waxing phase approaching the early degrees of a water sign in the sidereal sky, carrying the quality of Purva Ashadha's invincibility and aspiration. For your Sagittarius Ascendant, the 5th house is governed by Mars, and the 11th house — where your natal Moon sits in Swati nakshatra — is governed by Venus. Swati is the nakshatra of the independent, wind-blown flame: it is the nakshatra of freedom-seekers, of those who stand alone in the open field and find that solitude is not emptiness but spaciousness. Your natal Moon in Swati, under Rahu's governance, has always been reaching — reaching toward community, toward the collective vision, toward the friend who becomes a co-conspirator in some larger purpose. Today, the sky affirms that reaching. The 11th house gains are available, but they come through authenticity rather than strategy.

Saturn transits in Aquarius in the sidereal sky — your 3rd house from Sagittarius Ascendant. The 3rd house is courage, communication, the hands and the voice and the sibling-relationships of the soul. Saturn here is the discipline of the communicator — the demand that you not merely speak, but that you speak what is structurally true, what will endure beyond the moment of its utterance. This transit through your 3rd house is building, over these months, a different kind of voice in you — slower, more precise, more willing to be misunderstood in service of being accurate. Your natal Saturn retrograde in Aquarius in the Shatabhisha nakshatra amplifies this: Shatabhisha is the nakshatra of the healer of hidden things, the one who finds the cure no one else could see. Trust what you know that cannot yet be fully named.
