Mercury enters Cancer today — its home sign for you in a deeper sense than for most. With your natal Mercury already placed in Cancer in the 6th house, this transit is less an arrival than a reunion: the planet of mind and language steps back into the emotional register your nervous system already knows. For a counselor and integrative thinker, this is extraordinary territory. Your therapeutic instincts sharpen. The way you listen, the way you name a client's experience, the way you locate meaning in what others call ordinary detail — all of this becomes more finely tuned. Mercury will remain in Cancer through August 9th, retrograding from June 29th onward. You are entering a sustained season of inward-facing thought, and today is its first morning.

The Moon is currently in Sagittarius, which forms an opposition to transiting Mercury just entering Cancer — a sky-level tension between the drive to range wide and the need to go deep. With your natal Moon in Pisces in the 2nd house, you may feel this as a mild internal friction: part of you wants to expand, to seek the philosophical overview, to keep moving through ideas. Another part — the watery, receptive part that governs your sense of material and emotional security — wants to settle, to feel, to return to something essential. Neither impulse is wrong. The Sagittarius Moon's fire wants motion; your Piscean instinct toward devotion pulls the other way. Today asks you to honor both without forcing a resolution.

Your Capricorn Ascendant, flanked by both Uranus and Neptune in the 1st house natally, gives you an outer bearing that can appear more certain and composed than your interior actually is. Mercury entering Cancer today activates the tension between that composed Capricorn face and the tender Cancerian inner world you carry behind it. With the Sun also in Cancer in the 6th, there is a call today around the work of caring — not just the craft of counseling or business, but the willingness to bring your own emotional intelligence into contact with what you build. Something you have been thinking carefully about — perhaps a program, a framework, a communication you have held back — may now feel ready to soften into language.

Your dharma in this life involves standing at the threshold between people and their own depths; the sky today endorses that work.
